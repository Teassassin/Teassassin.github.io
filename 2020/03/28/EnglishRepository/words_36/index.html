百词斩 雅思 2020/03/28

total 14 words

sustenance

  • n. 生计
  • the food and the drink that people, animals and plants need to live and stay healthy
  • exp. Picking up rubbish is a vital source of sustenance for those poor tramps.

safeguard

  • v. 保护，维护；
  • to protect something / somebody from loss, harm or damage; to keep something / somebody safe
  • exp. The job of the guards is to safeguard the money.
  • n. 防卫措施，防范措施

arena

  • n. 竞技场，角斗场；(活动、斗争或竞争的) 场所，战场，(竞争) 舞台
  • a place with a flat open area in the middle and seats around it where people can watch sports and entertainment
  • exp. The Rome colosseum was an arena built in ancient times.

sarcasm

  • n. 讽刺，挖苦，嘲笑
  • a way of using words that are the opposite of what you mean in order to be unpleasant to somebody or to make fun of them
  • exp. I like her talk show; her sarcasm is funny and lightens our mood.

anonymous

  • a. 匿名的；无名的；不具名的；无特色的；无个性特征
  • written, given, made, etc. by somebody who does not want their name to be known or made public
  • exp. I received an anonymous letter. I did not know who wrote it.

ideology

  • n. 意识形态，思想 (体系)
  • a set of ideas that an economic or political system is based on; a set of beliefs, especially one held by a particular group, that influences the way people behave
  • exp. The animals formed two opposite ideologies based on what they eat.

chronological

  • a. 按照时间顺序排列的
  • (of a number of events) arranged in the order in which they happened
  • exp. The list of these books is in chronological order.

catalyst

  • n. 催化剂；造成变化的人或事
  • a substance that makes a chemical reaction happen faster without being changed itself
  • exp. Catalysts help chemical reactions happen faster.

intuition

  • n. 直觉；感觉
  • the ability to know something by using your feelings rather than considering the facts
  • exp. Her intuition told her that something was wrong.

plight

  • n. 困境；
  • a difficult and sad situation
  • exp. Drifting on the sea for days without rescue, he fell into a desperate plight.
  • v. 宣誓，保证

wedge

  • n. 楔子；
  • a piece of wood, rubber, metal, etc. with one thick end and one thin pointed end that you use to keep a door open, to keep two things apart, or to split wood or rock
  • exp. I drove a wedge through a log to split it in two.
  • v. 楔入

charter

  • n. 宪章；特权，豁免权；租赁；
  • a written statement describing the rights that a particular group of people should have
  • exp. The American Constitution is a charter that outlines a system of government.
  • v. 包，租 (飞机、汽车等)；
  • a. 包租的，租用的

illustrate

  • v. 举例说明，阐明
  • to make the meaning of something clearer by using examples, pictures, etc.
  • exp. Our manager illustrated the sales with colorful charts.

dose

  • n. (药) 剂量，用量，一剂；
  • the amount of a medicine, drug, or vitamin that is taken at one time
  • exp. You should be careful with the dose of this medicine.
  • vt. 给···服药
