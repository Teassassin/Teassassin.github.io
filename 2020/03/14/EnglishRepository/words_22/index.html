百词斩 雅思 2020/03/14

total 20 words

fixture

  • n. 固定装置
  • a thing such as a bath / bathtub or a toilet that is fixed in a house and that you do not take with you when you move house
  • exp. The light fixture is being installed.

poultry

  • n. 家禽(肉)
  • chickens, ducks and geese, kept for their meat or eggs; meat from chickens, ducks and geese
  • exp. Farmers keep poultry, such as chickens and ducks, for their meat and eggs.

alloy

  • n. 合金；
  • a metal that is formed by mixing two types of metal together, or by mixing metal with another substance
  • exp. We used a strong alloy for the blades of the engine.
  • v. 使成合金

meadow

  • n. 草地
  • a field covered in grass, used especially for hay
  • exp. There are a flock of sheep wandering around the meadow.

gust

  • n. 阵风，(感情的) 迸发，汹涌
  • a sudden strong increase in the amount and speed of wind that is blowing; a sudden strong expression of emotion
  • exp. The gust of wind almost blew her away.

toxic

  • a. 有毒的；
  • containing poison; poisonous
  • exp. The air is toxic; Don’t breathe it.
  • n. 毒物

garment

  • n. 服装
  • a piece of clothing
  • exp. Which garment should I choose to wear today?

redundant

  • a. 多余的，过剩的
  • not needed or useful
  • exp. The old steam train is redundant and has been replaced with newer trains.

panorama

  • /ˌpænə’ræmə/
  • n. 全景，概观
  • a view of a wide area of land; a description, study or set of pictures that presents all the different aspects or stages of a particular subject, event, etc.
  • exp. This panorama shows the entire downtown skyline.

nurture

  • v. 培植；养育，培育；
  • to care for and protect somebody / something while they are growing and developing
  • exp. The plant needs to be carefully nurtured with water and fertilizer.
  • n. 营养物

repent

  • v. 悔改；后悔
  • to feel or show that you are sorry for something bad or wrong that you did
  • exp. He repents his stupid decision.

whip

  • vt. 鞭打，抽打；
  • to hit a person or an animal hard with a whip, as a punishment or to make them go faster or work harder
  • exp. The guy whips his horses to make them run faster.
  • n. 组织秘书；
  • v. 一下子猛地移动，急走，急冲

judicial

  • a. 司法的，法庭的，法律的，审判 (上) 的
  • connected with a court, a judge or legal judgement
  • exp. The judge, jury and witnesses are important participants in the judicial system.

grumble

  • v. 发牢骚；
  • to complain about somebody / something in a bad-tempered way; to make a deep continuous sound
  • exp. My mom always grumbles about the mess as she cleans my room.
  • n. 抱怨，怨言

bibliography

  • n. 参考书目；文献学
  • a list of books or articles about a particular subject or by a particular author; the list of books, etc. that have been used by somebody writing an article, etc.
  • exp. The bibliography is located at the end of the paper.

tackle

  • v. 对付，处理；(足球等比赛中) 阻截；
  • to make a determined effort to deal with a difficult problem or situation
  • exp. The repairman is able to tackle any problems with his tools.
  • n. 阻截

vicinity

  • n. 附近地区；近邻
  • the area around a particular place
  • exp. These houses are in the vicinity of the hospital.

intrigue

  • n. 阴谋；诡计；
  • the activity of making secret plans in order to achieve an aim, often by tricking people
  • exp. An evil intrigue suddenly comes to his mind.
  • v. 密谋；欺骗；引起极大兴趣

bust

  • n. 半身塑像；胸部；
  • a stone or metal model of a person’s head, shoulders and chest
  • exp. The bust of Pythagoras honors the great Greek mathematician.
  • v. 打碎；搜捕；降级；终止；
  • a. 破产的；打碎的

porch

  • n. 门廊；走廊
  • a small area at the entrance to a building, such as a house or a church, that is covered by a roof and often has walls; a platform with an open front and a roof, built onto the side of a house on the ground floor
  • exp. I love to sit on the front porch and feel the wind.
