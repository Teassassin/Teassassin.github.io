百词斩 雅思 2020/03/02

total 17 words

frugal

  • a. 节俭的，俭朴的，不昂贵的，廉价的，花钱少的，不充足的
  • careful about spending money or using things when you do not need to : using money or supplies in a very careful way
  • exp. He’s such a frugal person who always mends his old clothes.

maltreat

  • v. 虐待
  • to be very cruel to a person or an animal
  • exp. The man with a belt in his hand maltreated his son.

crevice

  • n. 缺口，裂缝
  • a narrow opening or crack in a hard surface and especially in rock
  • exp. There is a deep crevice in the ground; make sure not to fall in.

lucid

  • a. 明晰的；头脑清楚的
  • able to be seen through with clarity
  • exp. The professor gives a lucid presentation about how to put an elephant in a fridge.

mansion

  • n. 官邸；巨宅；大厦
  • a large impressive house
  • exp. Look at how huge that mansion is!

stoop

  • v. 弯腰，俯身；
  • to walk or stand with your head and shoulders bent forward
  • exp. It is amazing that they could stoop so low.
  • n. 弯腰

inert

  • a. 不动的，惰性的；行动迟钝的
  • (chemistry) without active chemical or other properties (= characteristics); (formal) without power to move or act
  • exp. These are the common inert gases.

bring forward

  • v. 提出；提前
  • to talk about or show (something) so that it can be seen or discussed by others
  • exp. She is always bringing forward new ideas in meetings.

mishap

  • n. 不幸的事，灾祸
  • a small mistake or amount of bad luck
  • exp. The boy suffered a series of mishaps on the way to school.

equitable

  • a. 公正的，公平的
  • fair and reasonable; treating everyone in an equal way
  • exp. The white rabbit proposed an equitable distribution of the carrots.

have one’s hands full

  • phr. 忙得不可开交；手头事情应接不暇
  • keep busy with sth.
  • exp. The mother has her hands full with both her work and her children.

persevere with

  • v. 坚持，不屈不挠
  • be persistent, refuse to stop
  • exp. If you persevere with your exercise, you can get in shape.

melodious

  • a. 音调悦耳的
  • having a pleasing melody
  • exp. The angel’s song is pretty and melodious.

wail

  • vi. 恸哭；悲叹；
  • to make a loud, long cry of sadness or pain
  • exp. The baby began to wail when he woke up and his mom wasn’t there.
  • n. 恸哭；
  • vt. 哀悼

overture

  • n. 主动表示，姿态，前奏曲，序言，事件的开端，提出问题；
  • a piece of music played at the start of an opera, a musical play, etc.
  • exp. The curtain was opened as the orchestra played the overture.
  • vt. 作为一种主动姿态提出，向···提出建议

pamper

  • v. 纵容，过分关怀，精心照料
  • to treat (someone or something) very well : to give (someone or something) a lot of attention and care
  • exp. It’s very common to see the grandparents pamper their grandson.

grudge

  • n. 妒嫉；忿忿不平；
  • a strong feeling of anger toward someone that lasts for a long time
  • exp. The queen held a grudge against Snow White because of her beauty.
  • v. 不情愿做
