百词斩 雅思 2020/03/23

total 25 words

hinder

  • v. 阻碍，妨碍
  • to make it difficult for somebody to do something or for something to happen
  • exp. The obstacle really hinders my forward progress.

impetus

  • n. 推动力；促进
  • the force or energy with which something moves
  • exp. His boss’s praise gives him the impetus to work harder.

prone

  • a. 易于···的；有···倾向的；俯卧的
  • likely to suffer from something or to do something bad
  • exp. Dogs are prone to pee indoors.

monotonous

  • a. 单调的，无聊的
  • never changing and therefore boring
  • exp. My breakfast routine is so monotonous; I eat steamed bread every day.

locomotive

  • n. 机车，火车头
  • a railway engine that pulls a train
  • exp. Old locomotives ran on coal and steam.

breach

  • n. 裂痕；破裂；冲击；违反；
  • a break in a relationship between people or countries
  • exp. The situation became very dangerous because of the breach in the dam.
  • v. 使有裂口；破坏

ensue

  • v. 跟着发生，接踵而来
  • to happen after or as a result of another event
  • exp. Landslide ensued after the storm.

perish

  • vi. 死去；
  • (of people or animals) to die, especially in a sudden violent way
  • exp. A family of three ducks perished in the fire.
  • v. 脆裂，老化，腐烂

lateral

  • a. 侧面的；横向的；
  • connected with the side of something or with movement to the side
  • exp. I love your profile - I want a picture with a lateral view of you.
  • n. 侧面部分

blunder

  • n. 愚蠢之举；大错；
  • a stupid or careless mistake
  • exp. He forgot to bring his parachute! What a terrible blunder that is!
  • vi. 犯大错；
  • v. 失策；绊倒

lump

  • n. 肿块；粗笨的人；
  • a swelling under the skin, sometimes a sign of serious illness
  • exp. There is a lump on his neck.
  • v. 成团，成堆；使成块；
  • n. 一小方块 (糖)

diminish

  • v. 减少；失势，削弱···的权势，减弱···的权势
  • to become or to make something become smaller, weaker, etc.
  • exp. This is terrible! Our profits are still diminishing, why?

glamour

  • n. 魅力；诱惑力
  • physical beauty that also suggests wealth or success
  • exp. Her glamour impresses me every time I see her.

shaft

  • n. 柄；轴；杆状物；
  • the long narrow part of an arrow, hammer, golf club, etc.
  • exp. A croquet mallet has a thin shaft and a heavy head.
  • vt. 给···装杆；
  • v. 苟刻地对待

sightseeing

  • n. 游览；观光
  • the activity of visiting the famous or interesting places of an area
  • exp. I like to go sightseeing around seaside towns.

pendulum

  • n. 摆，钟摆；摇摆不定的事态 (或局面)
  • a long straight part with a weight at the end that moves regularly from side to side to control the movement of a clock
  • exp. The pendulum of the clock swung back and forth.

augment

  • v. 增加；助长
  • to increase the amount, value, size, etc. of something
  • exp. He augmented his income through freelance writing/

crumble

  • v. 弄碎；使破碎；崩溃；破灭；
  • to break or break something into very small pieces
  • exp. He crumbled the snack noodle into pieces before eating it.
  • n. 碎屑；面包屑

scrap

  • n. 废金属；小块，小片；碎片；口角，吵架；
  • a small piece of something, especially paper, cloth, etc.
  • exp. All these old cars will be used as scrap.
  • v. 报废；舍弃；打架

slum

  • n. 贫民窟，贫民区，陋巷
  • an area of a city that is very poor and where the houses are dirty and in bad condition
  • exp. The living conditions in this slum are shockingly poor.

instantaneous

  • a. 瞬间的；即刻的；猝发的
  • happening immediately
  • exp. When stabbing the balloon, there was an instantaneous burst.

imperative

  • a. 必要的；祈使的；急需的；
  • very important and needing immediate attention or action
  • exp. It is imperative to call 119 if your house is on fire.
  • n. 命令；必要的事；义务；驱使

plunge

  • v. 跳进，(使) 投入；使陷入；暴跌；
  • to move or make somebody / something move suddenly forwards and / or downwards; to move up and down suddenly and violently
  • exp. The sportswoman plunged into the water, splash!

manure

  • n. 肥料；
  • the waste matter from animals that is spread over or mixed with the soil to help plants and crops grow
  • exp. The farmer adds manure to the crops to help them grow.
  • v. 施肥于

ingenious

  • a. 聪明的；有发明天才的；有独创性的，别出心裁的
  • (of a person) having a lot of clever new ideas and good at inventing things
  • exp. He’s an ingenious boy who always has wonderful ideas.
