百词斩 雅思 2020/03/11

total 28 words

debris

  • n. 废墟，残骸
  • pieces of wood, metal, brick, etc. that are left after something has been destroyed; rubbish / garbage or pieces of material that are left somewhere and are not wanted
  • exp. The debris from buildings can be seen everywhere after the earthquake.

cylinder

  • n. 圆柱体，圆筒；汽缸，泵 (或桶) 体
  • a solid or hollow figure with round ends and long straight sides; an object shaped like a cylinder, especially one used as a container
  • exp. Do you know how to calculate the volume of a cylinder?

signify

  • vt. 表示；
  • to be a sign of something
  • exp. Traffic signs signify different things you must do on the road.
  • vi. 具有重要性

deficit

  • n. 亏损；赤字；不足额
  • the amount by which money spent or owed is greater than money earned in a particular period of time
  • exp. The company has been in a financial deficit for the past few years.

procure

  • vt. (努力) 取得；
  • to get (something) by some action or effort
  • exp. I finally procured a signed photo of my idol!
  • vi. 介绍娼妓

venerate

  • v. 崇敬，敬仰
  • to have and slow a lot of respect for somebody / something, especially somebody / something that is considered to be holy or very important
  • exp. The boss contributes a lot for the company, making his employees venerate him.

freight

  • n. 货运；运费；
  • the carrying of goods by some means of transport
  • exp. The freight train transports the goods.
  • v. 运送

seclude

  • v. 与世隔绝，使隔绝
  • to keep (someone) away from other people
  • exp. She secluded herself from the outside world, both alone and lonely.

dividend

  • n. (股份的) 红利，回报，效益，被除数
  • an amount of the profits that a company pays to people who own shares in the company; a money prize that is given to winners in the football pools; a number which is to be divided by another number
  • exp. The company paid dividends to its investors.

check out

  • phr. 结账后离开，检验，通过考核，合格
  • announce one’s departure from a hotel
  • exp. She has to check out at noon/

spouse

  • n. 夫，妻；配偶，夫妇
  • a husband or wife
  • exp. My spouse and I have been married for many years.

invoice

  • n. 发票；
  • a list of goods that have been sold, work that has been done, etc., showing what you must pay
  • exp. This is an invoice for your lunch bills.
  • v. 开发票；记清单

gush

  • v. 涌出，滔滔不绝地说，洋洋洒洒地写；
  • to flow out very quickly and in large amounts
  • exp. The water is gushing out of the broken fire hydrant.
  • n. 迸发

telling

  • a. 明显的；生动的
  • giving information about someone or something without intending to
  • exp. It is telling that she was sick.

bulge

  • n. 凸出部分；
  • a rounded lump on the surface of something
  • exp. He has a bulge in his stomach from drinking too much beer.
  • v. 凸出，膨胀

manuscript

  • n. 手稿；底稿；手抄本
  • a copy of a book, piece of music, etc. before it has been printed
  • exp. This is the manuscript of a famous writer.

alleviate

  • v. 减轻；缓解；缓和
  • exp. This kind of medicine could alleviate my toothache.

notch

  • n. 刻痕，凹口；
  • a small cut that is shaped like a V and that is a V and that is made on an edge or a surface
  • exp. For each day he spent on the island, he marked a new notch on the tree.
  • v. 在···上刻凹痕

statute

  • n. 法令，法规，条例
  • a written law that is formally created by a government
  • exp. The government will pass this statute.

assimilate

  • v. 使同化；吸收；消化
  • to become, or allow somebody to become, a part of a country or community rather than remaining in a separate group
  • exp. These business people have all been assimilated into a n identical-looking crowd.

concoct

  • v. 调和；编造 (故事等)
  • to make (a food or drink) by mixing different things together
  • exp. He is concocting an unusual drink.

buck

  • n. 雄鹿；年轻男士；美元；
  • a male deer, hare or rabbit (also called a buck rabbit)
  • exp. The buck is standing in the snow.
  • v. 反对；马弓背踢后腿挑起

valve

  • n. 阀门，活门
  • a device for controlling the flow of a liquid or gas, letting it move in one direction only; a structure in the heart or in a vein that lets blood flow in one direction only; a device in some brass musical instruments for changing the note
  • exp. Loosen the valve so the water can flow through.

thrust

  • v. 猛冲，猛推；
  • to push something / somebody suddenly or violently in a particular direction; to move quickly and suddenly in a particular direction
  • exp. The rocket thrusts high into the sky.
  • n. 主要观点

deft

  • a. 灵巧的，敏捷的，熟练的
  • skillful and clever
  • exp. Mice are deft with their hands, and can easily escape from their cages.

stem

  • n. 茎，(树) 干；
  • the main long thin part of a plant above the ground from which the leaves or flowers grow; a smaller part that grows from this and supports flowers or leaves
  • exp. The boy is climbing along the stem to reach the clouds.
  • v. 起源于；阻止

pass over

  • v. 忽略，不注意
  • bypass
  • exp. The short player was passed over in ffavor of the tall players.

be bent on

  • v. 专心致志于
  • having a strong desire to do (something)
  • exp. She is bent on her online assignments and thinks about the answers.
