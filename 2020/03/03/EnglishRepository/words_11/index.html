百词斩 雅思 2020/03/03

total 15 words

go on about sth.

  • v. 喋喋不休
  • chatter without stop
  • exp. The professor started going on about something again, so I just left.

fraction

  • n. 部分，片段，碎片；分数
  • a small part or amount of something
  • exp. The biggest fraction of the chart is shown in red.

inundate

  • v. 淹没，泛滥
  • to cover (something) with a flood of water
  • exp. The streets are inundated with water.

assert

  • v. 坚持；断言；肯定地说；维护
  • to state clearly and firmly that something is true
  • exp. They both assert their opposing opinions. Who should I believe?

lottery

  • n. 彩票；碰运气的事，难算计的事
  • a way of raising money for a government, charity, etc. by selling tickets that have different numbers on them that people have chosen. Numbers are then chosen by chance and the people who have those numbers on their tickets win prizes
  • exp. He was checking numbers on the TV to see if he would win the lottery.

ruffle

  • vt. 使起波浪，扰乱，激怒；
  • to move or lift (something) so that it is no longer smooth
  • exp. A light breeze ruffled the surface of the water.
  • n. 荷叶边，花边

embark

  • v. 开始，着手，从事；上船或飞机
  • to get onto a ship; to put something onto a ship
  • exp. Columbus embarked on his journey to America in 1492.

quota

  • n. 限额；配额；定额
  • the limited number or amount of people or things that is officially allowed
  • exp. He was told that the quota of liquor is two bottles per person.

soak

  • v. 浸泡，渗透，吸入；
  • to put something in liquid for a time so that it becomes completely wet; to become completely wet in this way
  • exp. The clothes should soak for a while before washing.

phobia

  • n. 恐惧症
  • an extremely strong dislike or fear of someone or something
  • exp. I have a phobia of heights, so my legs keep trembling from fear.

redeem

  • vt. 弥补；赎回
  • to make somebody / something seem less bad
  • exp. To try and redeem the situation of the stolen sheep, he fixed the broken fence.

monarchy

  • n. 君主政体
  • a country that is ruled by a monarch (such as a king or queen)
  • exp. In a monarchy, the country is ruled by a King or a Queen.

cast sb. (sth.) aside

  • v. 抛弃，废除
  • put an end to a state or an activity
  • exp. He decided to cast his cigarettes aside and quit smoking.

do away with

  • phr. 废除，消灭，去掉
  • terminate or take out
  • exp. A number of countries do away with the death penalty.
