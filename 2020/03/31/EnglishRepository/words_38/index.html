百词斩 雅思 2020/03/31

total 12 words

outskirts

  • n. (尤指城市) 郊区
  • the parts of a town or city that are furthest from the center
  • exp. The areas outside of the fifth ring road are outskirts.

virtuous

  • a. 善良的，有德行的，贞洁的
  • behaving in a very good and moral way; claiming to behave better or have higher moral standards than other people
  • exp. It’s virtuous to help people in need.

dean

  • n. (大学的) 学院院长；系主任；牧师
  • (in a college or university, especially at Oxford or Cambridge) a person who is responsible for the discipline of students
  • exp. The dean of our department is reading in his office.

clause

  • n. 条款；从句
  • an item in a legal document that says that a particular thing must or must not be done
  • exp. The tenant will sign the tenancy agreement after checking every clause.

wicked

  • a. 邪恶的，恶毒的
  • morally bad
  • exp. This wicked man is slapping the elderly disabled man.

postgraduate

  • n. 研究生；
  • a person who already holds a first degree and who is doing advanced study or research; a graduate student
  • exp. I have completed two years of postgraduate study and still have one year to go.
  • a. 大学毕业后的，研究生的

ramble

  • vi. 瞎扯；溜达；
  • to talk about somebody / something in a confused way, especially for a long time
  • exp. He rambled on and on until everyone was bored.
  • n. 漫步；
  • vi. 漫步于

exceptional

  • a. 优越的，杰出的；异常的
  • unusually good
  • exp. Wow, what an exceptional athlete! Look at all those trophies!

brochure

  • n. 手册，小册子
  • a small magazine or book containing pictures and information about something or advertising something
  • exp. She carefully studied the company’s brochure.

sheer

  • a. 陡峭的；
  • very steep
  • exp. The rocky cliff over the ocean is so sheer!
  • v. (off) 急转向，偏离；
  • n. 透明薄纱，透明布料的衣服

shatter

  • v. 粉碎；打碎；使粉碎；砸碎
  • to suddenly break into small pieces; to make something suddenly break into small pieces
  • exp. The lotion bottle hit the floor and shattered into pieces!

cemetery

  • n. 墓地；公墓
  • Many tombstones lie in this cemetery.
  • exp. an area of land used for burying dead people, especially one that is not beside a church
