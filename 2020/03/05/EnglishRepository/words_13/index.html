百词斩 雅思 2020/03/05

total 17 words

delinquency

  • n. 【法律】少年犯罪，不法行为 (青少年) ，行为不当
  • exp. The delinquency is showing an increasing trend in many countries.

prompt

  • v. 提示；激起；促进，推动；敦促；
  • to encourage somebody to speak by asking them questions or suggesting words that they could say; to make somebody decide to do something; to cause something to happen;
  • exp. My phone prompts me to get out of bed and finish my paper!
  • a. 迅速的，及时的；
  • n. 提示

erratic

  • a. 反复无常的，不稳定的
  • acting, moving, or changing in ways that are not expected or usual : not consistent or regular

contend

  • v. 搏斗；争辩；坚决主张
  • to compete against somebody in order to gain something
  • exp. The two fighters contend for the championship.

metaphor

  • n. 暗喻，隐喻
  • a word or phrase used to describe somebody / something else, in a way that is different from its normal use, in order to show that the two things have the same qualities and to make the description more powerful, for example She has a heart of stone; the use of such words and phrases
  • exp. We use a metaphor in this sentence: “She is a walking dictionary”.

inhabit

  • v. 居住于
  • to live in a particular place
  • exp. Primitive tribes used to inhabit these forests.

culminate

  • v. 使达高潮，使结束
  • to end with a particular result, or at a particular point
  • exp. The marathon culminates in a winner.

to one’s face

  • phr. 当面；坦白地
  • in one’s presence
  • exp. She wanted to say sorry to her face, rather than sending a message.

prospectus

  • n. 章程，内容简介
  • a printed statement that descibes something (such as a new business or investment) and that is sent to people who may want to be involved in it or invest in it
  • exp. Please read this prospectus to see if you want to invest in our company.

annuity

  • n. 年金，养老金，年金保险，年金享受全
  • an insurance policy or an investment that pays someone a fixed amount of money each other
  • exp. The bank pays me a small annuity payment every year.

evoke

  • v. 使人想起；唤起；引起
  • to bring a feeling, a memory or an image into your mind
  • exp. This picture evokes feelings of summer.

convict

  • n. 犯人
  • a person who has been found guilty of a crime and sent to prison
  • v. (经审讯) 证明···有罪，使···知罪

ferry

  • n. 渡船，渡轮；渡口；
  • a boat or ship that carries people, vehicles and goods across a river or across a narrow part of the sea
  • v. 运送；渡运 (人、车或物等)

hand sth. down to sb.

  • v. (作为传统) 传下，传给
  • passed on, as by inheritance
  • exp. I handed my suit down to my son; it’s so big on him!

contemplate

  • v. 沉思；对···周密考虑；注视
  • to think about whether you should do something or how you should do something
  • exp. I contemplate the meaning of life on the toilet.

aviation

  • n. 航空，航空学；飞机制造业
  • the designing, building and flying of aircraft
  • exp. Aviation is one of my biggest interests-I love airplanes.

bitumen

  • n. 沥青
  • any of various naturally occurring impure mixtures of hydrocarbons
  • exp. Highways are covered in bitumen.
