百词斩 雅思 2020/03/17

total 25 words

errand

  • n. 差事；差使；使命
  • a job that you do for somebody that involves going somewhere to take a message, to buy something, deliver goods, etc.
  • exp. In many companies, interns run boring errands for their bosses.

frail

  • a. 脆弱的，不坚实的
  • (especially of an old person) physically weak and thin; weak; easily damaged or broken
  • exp. The frail old lady needs a walking stick to walk.

inferior

  • a. (等级、地位等) 下等的，低等的；低劣的；
  • (formal) of lower rank; lower
  • exp. He makes me fell I’m inferior to him.
  • n. 部下，地位低的人；次品

chamber

  • n. 会议室
  • a hall in a public building that is used for formal meetings
  • exp. The chamber has enough seats for a meeting.

peel

  • v. 剥皮，削皮，去壳；
  • exp. You must peel the banana before you eat it.
  • n. 果皮，外皮

unanimous

  • a. 一致同意的；全体一致的
  • if a decision or an opinion is unanimous, it is agreed or shared by everyone in a group
  • exp. There was unanimous agreement that we should do the business deal.

exemplify

  • v. 举例说明；例示，是···的典范
  • to give an example in order to make something clearer
  • exp. The teacher uses her gesture to exemplify the pronunciations in English.

endorse

  • v. 代言
  • to say in an advertisement that you use and like a particular product so that other people will want to buy it
  • exp. The celebrity is endorsing the brand’s lipstick.

deduct

  • v. 减去
  • to take away money, points, etc. from a total amount
  • exp. Two points will be deducted for a wrong answer.

irony

  • n. 具有讽刺意味的事；反语；冷嘲
  • the amusing or strange aspect of a situation that is very different from what you expect; a situation like this
  • exp. I slipped right next to the “do not slip” sign, and we all laughed at the irony.

likewise

  • adv. 同样地，此外
  • the same; in a similar way; also
  • exp. The big man hates the small man; likewise, the small man hates the big man.

cellar

  • n. 酒窖；地窖；地下室
  • an underground room where wine is stored; the wine stored in this room
  • exp. The cellar is filled with wine barrels.

cradle

  • n. 摇篮；策源地，发源地；
  • a small bed for a baby which can be pushed gently from side to side
  • exp. The adorable baby is lying in the cradle.
  • v. 轻轻地抱，捧

reciprocal

  • a. 相互的，互惠的
  • involving two people or groups who agree to help each other or behave in the same way to each other
  • exp. Reciprocal relationships contribute to a good neighborhood.
    互惠关系有助于建立好的邻里感情。

incorporate

  • v. 包含；使并入
  • exp. The app update incorporates many new features and improvements.

allege

  • v. 断言；硬说
  • to state something as a fact but without giving proof
  • exp. Some people allege that these red envelopes were given by Cantonese people.

pertinent

  • a. 有关的；适当的；中肯的
  • appropriate to a particular situation
  • exp. I collected pertinent information of France before I traveled there.

booth

  • n. 有篷的售货摊；小舍；隔开的小间；公用电话亭；窝棚
  • a small confined place where you can do something privately, for example make a telephone call, or vote
  • exp. I buy a piece of pancake from the booth every morning.

remedy

  • v. 治疗；恢复；补救；
  • to relieve or cure (a disease, illness, etc.) by or as if by a remedy
  • exp. The liar claimed that his medicine can remedy all diseases.
  • n. 治疗；补救；矫正

glitter

  • v. 闪闪发光，闪耀；
  • to shine brightly with little flashes of light, like a diamond; (of the eyes) to shine brightly with a particular emotion, usually a strong one
  • exp. Look at the crystal chandelier glittering. It’s so beautiful!
  • n. 灿烂的光辉，闪光

synthetic

  • a. 合成的，人造的；
  • artificial; made by combining chemical substances rather than being produced naturally by plants or animals
  • exp. These fibers are synthetic.
  • n. 合成物

comprise

  • v. 构成；包含，包括
  • to have somebody / something as parts or members
  • exp. These animals comprise most of the cat family.

inflict

  • v. 使遭受 (损伤、痛苦等)
  • to make somebody / something suffer something unpleasant
  • exp. His illness inflicted sharp pain on his stomach.

weld

  • v. 焊接；使接合，使成整体；
  • to join pieces of metal together by heating their edges and pressing them together
  • exp. He is welding together the two pieces of metal.
  • n. 焊接

yacht

  • n. 游艇；帆船
  • a large sailing boat, often also with an engine and a place to sleep on board, used for pleasure trips and racing
  • exp. Let’s take our yacht out on the ocean today.
