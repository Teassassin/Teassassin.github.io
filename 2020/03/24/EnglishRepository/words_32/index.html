百词斩 雅思 2020/03/24

total 28 words

feasible

  • a. 可行的，行得通的
  • that is possible and likely to be achieved
  • exp. I think your construction plan is feasible. Let’s start to build the house!

convert

  • v. 可转变为，可变换成；改变，转变，转化；使改变信仰
  • to be able to be changed from one form, purpose or system to another
  • exp. Cool! The sofa can be easily converted into a bed.

martyr

  • n. 烈士；
  • a person who suffers very much or is killed because of their religious or political beliefs
  • exp. He died in the line of duty and was dubbed a martyr.
  • v. 迫害，折磨

tentative

  • a. 试探性的；不确定的；暂时的；犹豫不决的
  • not behaving or done with confidence
  • exp. When walking in darkness, every step you take is tentative.

condolence

  • n. 哀悼；吊唁
  • sympathy that you feel for somebody when a person in their family or that they know well has died; an expression of this sympathy
  • exp. People lit candles to show their condolence for the earthquake victims.

wholesale

  • n. 批发；
  • in large quantities, especially so they can be sold again to make a profit
  • exp. Retailers buy things in wholesale.
  • a. 大规模的；批发的

whilst

  • conj. 当···的时候；
  • at the same time as something else is happening; during the time that something is happening
  • exp. They were at their happiest whilst playing soccer.
  • n. 一会儿；一段时间

scandal

  • n. 丑闻；丢脸；流言蜚语；反感；愤慨
  • behavior or an event that people think is morally or legally wrong and causes public feelings of shock or anger
  • exp. Mr. Giraffe was involved in the academic scandal and received widespread media coverage.

sphere

  • n. 球；领域
  • any object that is completely round, for example a ball
  • exp. The world is not flat; it it a sphere.

commodity

  • n. 商品，货物
  • a product or a raw material that can be bought and sold; a thing that is useful or has a useful quality
  • exp. You can find many commodities in supermarkets.

attain

  • v. 获得；达到 (某个年纪、水平、条件) ；实现；
  • to succeed in getting something, usually after a lot of effort
  • exp. I finally attained my driving license!
  • n. 成就

definitive

  • a. 可靠的，明确的，权威的
  • considered to be the best of its kind and almost impossible to improve
  • exp. The new law is definitive; we now know exactly what it means.

incidence

  • n. 发生率，入射角度
  • the extent to which something happens or has an effect
  • exp. The incidence of heart disease goes up as people get older.

verdict

  • n. (陪审团) 裁决
  • a decision that is made by a jury in court, stating if somebody is considered guilty of a crime or not
  • exp. The judge is making his final verdict.

notion

  • n. 观点，看法；概念，观念；突然的念头；意图
  • an idea, a belief or an understanding of something
  • exp. Many Chinese have the notion that all French men are romantic.

voucher

  • n. 代金券；教育补助金券；
  • a printed piece of paper that can be used instead of money to pay for something, or that allows you to pay less than the usual price of something
  • exp. I won a voucher worth $5000, but only for purchasing the private jet.
  • vt. 证实···的可靠性

piston

  • n. 活塞，瓣
  • a part of an engine that consists of a short cylinder that fits inside a tube and moves up and down or backwards and forwards to make other parts of the engine move
  • exp. He is standing in a garage with a piston in his hands.

sophisticated

  • a. (仪器) 精密的，复杂的；老于世故的，老练的，富有经验的；高雅的，精致的
  • Someone is comfortable in social situations and knows about culture, fashion, and other matters that are considered socially important.
  • exp. This machine is quite sophisticated.

provoke

  • v. 激怒；对···挑衅
  • to say or do something that you know will annoy somebody so that they react in an angry way
  • exp. The basketball player’s dirty behavior really provoked his opponent.

commence

  • v. 开始
  • to begin to happen; to begin something
  • exp. We were about to commence the race.

intimate

  • a. 亲密的，密切的；详细的；
  • (of knowledge) very detailed and thorough
  • exp. Nothing is sweeter than intimate lovers.
  • v. 宣布，通知；
  • n. 密友，知己

reproach

  • v. 责备，指责；
  • to blame or criticize somebody for something that they have done or not done, because you are disappointed in them; to feel guilty about something that you think you should have done in a different way
  • exp. The master reproached the dog for destroying the sofa.
  • n. 指责

reconcile

  • v. 和解；调解；调和
  • one find an acceptable way of dealing with two or more ideas, needs, etc. that seem to be opposed to each other; to make people become friends again after an argument or a disagreement
  • exp. They are desperate to reconcile and fix their broken hearts.

implement

  • n. 工具；器具；
  • a tool or an instrument, often one that is quite simple and that is used outdoors
  • exp. I use these implements to take care of my garden.
  • v. 实施，执行

impart

  • v. 传授，给予，将 (某性质) 给予或赋予某事物
  • to pass information, knowledge, etc. to other people; to give a particular quality to something
  • exp. A teacher’s duty is to impart knowledge to students.

buffet

  • n. 自助餐，(火车内) 餐室，冷餐桌；
  • a meal at which people serve themselves from a table and then stand or sit somewhere else to eat
  • exp. You can eat as much as you like at the buffet.
  • v. 反复敲打，打击，重击

outset

  • n. 开始，开端
  • the start or beginning of something
  • exp. You need to be well prepared from the outset.

engrave

  • v. 雕刻
  • to cut words or designs on wood, stone, metal, etc.
  • exp. The artist is engraving patterns into the wood.
