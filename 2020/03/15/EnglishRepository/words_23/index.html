百词斩 雅思 2020/03/15

total 23 words 2 similar

practitioner

  • n. (医生或律师等) 开业者；实践者，从事者
  • a person who works in a profession, especially medicine or law
  • exp. A medical practitioner comforts the woman.

radical

  • n. 激进分子；
  • a person with radical opinions
  • exp. Some radicals insist that every animal should eat grass.
  • a. 激进的；重大的，彻底的

merit

  • n. 优点；价值；
  • the quality of being good and of deserving praise, reward or admiration; the quality of being deserving
  • exp. A merit of this phone is that the excellent camera can clearly capture the moon. (指华为P30)
  • v. 值得；应得；
  • n. 功绩

commend

  • v. 称赞；委托保管
  • to praise somebody / something, especially publicly
  • exp. The worker was highly commended for his hard work.
  • similar command

plunder

  • v. 抢劫，掠夺；
  • to steal things from a place, especially using force during a time of war
  • exp. A burglar broke in and plundered my house.
  • n. 抢劫，战利品

facilitate

  • v. 为他人提供方便；使便利；
  • to make an action or a process possible or easier
  • exp. The machine facilitates her ability to go places by herself.
  • vt. 促进

propagate

  • vt. 传播，宣传；
  • to spread an idea, a belief or a piece of information among many people
  • exp. We want to propagate our company’s influence across the world.
  • v. 繁殖，增殖

jury

  • n. 陪审团；全体评审员，评判委员会
  • a group of members of the public who listen to the facts of a case in a court and decide whether or not somebody is guilty of a crime
  • exp. The lawyer is showing the evidence to the jury.

reel

  • n. 卷盘；
  • a round object around which you wind such things as thread, wire or film; a reel together with the film, wire, thread, etc. that is wound around it
  • exp. Movies used to be stored on reels of film; now, they’re on hard drives.
  • v. 感到震惊；眩晕；旋转

verge

  • n. 边缘；边界；
  • (British English) a piece of grass at the edge of a path, road, etc.
  • exp. The verge of the road is covered with lovely blue flowers.
  • v. 接近；濒临

masculine

  • a. 男子气概的
  • of or belonging to the class of words (called a gender) that ordinarily includes most of the words referring to males
  • exp. His beard and suit are masculine enough to show his charm.

cue

  • n. 球杆；
  • an action or event that is a signal for somebody to do something
  • exp. While playing billiards, you should hit the ball with a cue.
    打台球时，你要用球杆去击球。
  • v. 给···提示

gown

  • n. 女礼服；女裙服；长袍
  • a woman’s dress, especially a long one for special occasions
  • exp. The bride wore aa beautiful white wedding gown.

curb

  • v. 勒住；抑制；控制；约束；
  • to control or limit something, especially something bad
  • exp. Curb your horse now before it runs off the cliff!
  • n. 路缘；控制；约束

juvenile

  • n. 青少年；雏鸟；
  • a young person who is not yet an addult
  • exp. This group of juveniles are taking a selfie together.
  • a. 青少年的；幼稚的

susceptible

  • a. 易受感染的；易受影响的；多情的
  • very likely to be influenced, harmed or affected by somebody / something
  • exp. Young children are susceptible to disease.

discrepancy

  • n. 不一致，差异，不符
  • a difference between two or more things that should be the same
  • exp. There is a noticeable discrepancy between the photo of the cake and the actual one.

formidable

  • a. 令人畏惧的；可怕的；难以克服的
  • if people, things or situations are formidable, you feel fear and / or respect for them, because they are impressive or powerful, or because they seem very difficult
  • exp. In ping-pong competition tournament, he was a formidable opponent.

converge

  • v. (向某一点) 相交，会合
  • (of two or more lines, paths, etc.) to move towards each other and meet at a point
  • exp. All the paths converged on a single point.

latitude

  • n. 纬度；(言论，行动的) 自由
  • the distance of a place north or south of the equator (= the line around the world diving north and south), measured in degrees
  • exp. The higher the latitude, the colder the temperature will be.

blend

  • v. 混合；把···混合一体；
  • to mix two or more substances together
  • exp. Blend the flour and the water with a spoon.
  • n. 混合物

stationery

  • n. 文具；(有配套信封的) 信纸
  • materials for writing and for using in an office, for example paper, pens and envelopes
  • exp. You can buy this nicely designed stationery from our online store.
  • similar stationary
    • a. 静止的；固定的
    • n. 定居者，驻军
