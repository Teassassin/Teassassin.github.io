大学英语四 Unit 1 Part II TextA

in the case of

  • as far as … is concerned
  • 至于，就···来说

stand / get / be in the way

  • prevent from doing sth.
  • 挡道；妨碍

be / get bogged down

  • become stuck and unable to make progress
  • bog
    • n. 沼泽；泥塘
    • vt. 使陷于泥沼；使动弹不得

engage / be engaged in (doing) sth.

  • 从事；参与

be faced with

  • have to deal with
  • 面临

press on / ahead

  • continue doing sth. in a determined way
  • (不顾困难)继续进行

bide one’s time

  • wait patiently for a chance
  • 等待时机

hit-and-run

  • a. hitting and then escaping
  • 打了就跑的

drag on

  • move slowly and with effort
  • continue endlessly and tediously
  • 缓慢费力地走；拖延

at the cost of

  • with the loss of
  • 以···为代价

catch sb. off guard

  • take sb. by surprise
  • 趁某人不备

bring to a halt

  • stop completely
  • 使停止

turn the tide (against)

  • change what looks like defeat into victory(over)
  • 彻底改变形势(从而对···不利)

reckon with

  • take into consideration
  • 考虑

take its / a toll

  • cause damage, injuries or deaths
  • 造成损失(伤亡等)

老师总结

assemble an army
集结军队

on the border of Russia
在俄罗斯的边界

a quick, decisive victory
速决速胜

retreat eastward
向东撤退

be faced with a crucial decision
面临着一个重要抉择

take the gamble of doing
冒险···

fierce battle
激战

by nightfall
夜幕降临时

bide one’s time
等待时机

turn into a nightmare
成为一场噩梦

froze to death
冻死

drag on
拖着脚步艰难行进

by a stroke of luck
侥幸的是

powerful alliance
强大的联盟

pride comes before a fall
骄兵必败

nothing could stand in their way
战无不胜

fierce resistance
奋勇抵抗

a raw, bitter, bleak Russian winter
阴冷凄苦的俄罗斯寒冬

military might
军事实力

launch an attack against…
向···发动进攻

a painful lesson
痛苦的教训

Without a declaration of war
不宣而战

Catch sb. off guard
被打了个措手不及

Despite great suffering
尽管受着极大的困苦

Food run out
食物耗尽

Die from hunger
饿死

Bring the Russian offensive to a halt
阻止了德国人的攻势

Launch two new offensives
发动两场新的进攻

The Red Army
红军

Heroic survivors
英勇无畏的幸存者

Farming region
农耕地区

Be liberated from the Germans
从德国手中解放出来
